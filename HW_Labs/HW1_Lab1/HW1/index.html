Design Choices

  • Different colors to represent which state was Democractic or Republican
  • Side bar to represent the states that don't have the state abbreviation
  • Legend elements to depict the essentials in the graph
  • Clicking on a state, gives more specific details about the candidates

I thought of this as a good design because I am able to understand the graph and am able to navigate it. I like the fact that the colors make sense in that Blue is for Democratic and Red is for Replublican instead of other obscure colors. I also like that fact that when you click on the state, it gives you more details that are easy to digest and doesn't take away from the graph.

I believe what the author is trying to convey is to let people see which state voted for who and how much of the vote was given to the candidate. With this, people are able to see how the election went more easily

Design Choices

Although not shown in the picture, you can click the play button and it gives you a visualization from 2004 to 2016 about the popularity of grapes in word searches on the internet. When you hover over a specific selection, you get more details about how many types the word grape was searched on Google that day.
